AR Glass

The AR Glass features an advanced anti-reflection (AR) coating that dramatically reduces glare and reflections, delivering a more vivid and eye-comfortable viewing experience.

The AR (Anti-Reflection) coating on this glass reduces surface reflection by up to 90% compared to standard glass, resulting in a display that looks brighter and clearer — especially outdoors. Ideal for premium smartphone lines and markets where display quality is a key selling point.
